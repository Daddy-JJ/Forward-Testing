FAQ

Common questions, clearly answered

A quick overview of what the platform is designed to support.

What is forward testing?

Forward testing observes how a strategy behaves after its rules are established, using market conditions that were not part of the original strategy development.

What can I organize in the platform?

The member workspace includes forward tests, strategies, a watchlist, trade journal, reports, portfolio context, profile, and billing.

Does the platform guarantee strategy performance?

No. Forward-testing records support analysis and review, but they do not guarantee future performance.

Can I compare strategy results?

The strategy, testing, dashboard, and reports modules are intended to keep performance evidence organized for review.

Is there an administrative workspace?

Yes. The approved product scope includes an Admin CMS for users, roles, permissions, pricing, subscriptions, content, analytics, media, and settings.
